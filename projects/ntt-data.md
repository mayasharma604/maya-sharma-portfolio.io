# NTT DATA — RFID Systems & Embedded Software Optimization

**Embedded Systems · Client Software · RFID · Performance Optimization · Systems Analysis**

Worked on software development and systems optimization for handheld RFID reader platforms used in mission‑critical field operations. The role focused on improving reliability, performance, and deployability of RFID‑based inventory and tracking systems across clinical and warehouse environments.

---

## Client Software Debugging & Performance Optimization

Identified and resolved severe battery drain issues in RFID handheld readers caused by inefficient client‑side software behavior.

**Technical contributions**
- Debugged low‑level client software interacting with RFID hardware and firmware
- Optimized polling, communication, and power‑management logic
- Reduced unnecessary background processes and inefficient I/O patterns

**Impact**
- Increased battery life by over **10×**, from ~45 minutes to ~8 hours
- Enabled sustained, all‑day use in field and clinical settings
- Significantly improved reliability for mission‑critical operations

---

## RFID Communication & Protocol Improvements

Enhanced the **Low Level Reader Protocol (LLRP)** software stack to improve communication efficiency between RFID tags and client applications.

**Software focus**
- Improved robustness and efficiency of tag–reader communication
- Reduced latency and unnecessary message overhead
- Strengthened client–hardware interaction under real‑world usage conditions

**Outcome**
- More stable and responsive RFID reads
- Improved system performance under continuous operation

---

## System Deployment & Field Integration

Supported deployment of the optimized RFID system across multiple real‑world environments.

**Deployment scope**
- Rolled out systems across **4 clinics and warehouses**
- Supported RFID‑based inventory tracking and asset management workflows

**Key considerations**
- Reliability under extended use
- Ease of integration with existing operational processes
- Performance consistency across environments

---

## Market & Systems Analysis

Conducted technical and market research on existing RFID handheld reader platforms to inform product and strategy decisions.

**Analysis work**
- Evaluated ~30 active handheld RFID readers
- Identified key performance indicators (KPIs) and usage trends
- Compared battery performance, communication reliability, and deployment constraints

**Outcome**
- Produced data‑driven insights to guide strategic recommendations
- Helped contextualize engineering tradeoffs within broader market constraints

- Ability to diagnose and fix real‑world performance bottlenecks
